Here are some of my experiments in data visualization or "data viz" as it's commonly known. I work primarily with R, a remarkably versatile open-source package used by statisticians, other social scientists and a handful of journalists. Comments are welcome.

Here's a more sophisticated map built with QGIS. It displays the supply of primary care physicians in Riverside and San Bernardino Counties. Outlying areas have long suffered from a severe shortage of doctors. Inland Empire Health Plan, the principal Medicaid contractor recently began offering $100,000 bonuses to doctors willing to relocate to the area.
